Digital Rock Physics And Remote Sensing

Supervisor: Dr. Irwan Ary Dharmawan, M.Si.

This platform is a dedicated hub for research and projects in the field of digital rock physics and remote sensing, carried out by students. It provides a detailed overview of various studies and innovations in these areas, offering insights into how advanced technologies and methods are applied to understand rock properties and monitor environmental changes. Users can explore comprehensive data, methodologies, and findings, all contributed by the student researchers involved in these cutting-edge fields.
